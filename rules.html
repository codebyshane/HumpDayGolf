General Information & League Rules

General Information

Where

Seminole Lake Country Club, 6100 Augusta Blvd. N, Seminole, FL

When
  • April 6th–September 21st
  • Wednesday Afternoons — 5:15 pm
  • End of Season Tournament — Saturday, September 24th
  • 25 Weeks
Scoring System
  • Individual and Team Play – 4 point system
  • Low hcp "A" vs. Low hcp "A" (net) – 1 point
  • High hcp "B" vs. High hcp "B" – 1 point
  • Total Team Low Net Score – 2 points
  • If a tie occurs – 1/2 points will be awarded.
  • All handicaps being the same, Team Captains will oppose each other.
  • Absent team members will lose their individual match (1 point) with the adjusted net score used in the Team Net Score Totals. Both team members are absent – a 4–point loss will occur.
Expectations of Members
  • Proper Golf attire at all times: No tee–shirts or cut–offs
  • Turn in your score slips in the bar/lounge – no extra holes may be played.
  • Turn in pre–printed match sheets with totals and signatures by each team after the 9–hole match is completed.
  • A forfeit will occur to each team without a completed slip for that night's match.
  • Upon completion of play, return all golf carts to the cart staging area. Do not leave carts in the parking area.

Rules of Play

Play by the rules of golf – if not listed.
DIVISION I & II

Play the ball as it lies – you may move and place it on the grass on hole #5 (front dirt area, & hole #8 (Left Rough Area – Find Grass). All Areas where loose dirt is spread.

DIVISION III & IV

Without penalty, you may move the ball up to 24" (except in hazard areas, behind trees, and the putting surface). You can improve your lie – not your line of flight or swing path.

Full League
  • All divisions play from the "White" tees – If your age and handicap add up to 80 or higher, you may play from the "red Tee Box.‚Äù This will aid in play enjoyment and a faster pace of play – you can not mix tees.
  • The maximum score on any hole is Triple Bogey (3 over par) – pick up your ball and move to the next hole.
  • Par 3 holes are cart path only – 90–degree rule.
  • No Carts within 30 yards of putting surface or surrounding mounds
  • Always repair your ball marks on greens / fill divots with sand
Out of Bounds
  • Cart path to hole #1 when hitting from #9 tee.
  • Maintenance roadway between holes #8 & #11
  • If the ball crosses or touches the roadway/cart path, its OB – 1 stroke penalty – not distance, keeps the pace of play.
Lateral Hazards

Play the ball as it lies, or take an unplayable lie, using two club lengths relief from hazard area – No closer to the hole – take one stroke penalty.

Water Hazards

If the ball goes in a water hazard, drop a ball where it crossed into the water, no closer to the hole. Take a one–stroke penalty. Cleaned–out material from ditches is considered ground under repair.

Free Relief
  • All planter bed areas, newly planted trees, fences and manmade objects, ground under repair areas, and tree roots (12" no closer to the hole).
  • Maintenance Area (holes #7, #8, #11) 2 club lengths from the area with no penalty.
  • With no penalty, you may move the ball from all footprints (in sand bunkers).
  • Any ball hitting a handrail/bridge must be replayed – no penalty
Substitutions

One (1) substitute per team is allowed to win points. No two (2) subs permitted on one (1) team to win points must designate one (1) team member absent.
