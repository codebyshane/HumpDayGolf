General Information & League Rules

General Information

Where

Seminole Lake Country Club, 6100 Augusta Blvd. N, Seminole, FL

When
  • April 6th–September 21st
  • Wednesday Afternoons — 5:15pm
  • End of Season Tournament — Saturday, September 24th
  • 25 Weeks
Scoring System
  • Individual and Team Play - 4 point system
  • Low hcp "A" vs. Low hcp "A" (net) - 1 point
  • High handicap "B" vs. High hcp "B" - 1 point
  • Total Team Low Net Score - 2 points
  • If a tie occurs - 1/2 points will be awarded.
  • All handicaps being the same, Team Captains will oppose each other.
  • Absent team members will lose their individual match (1 point) with adjusted net score used in the Team Net Score Totals. Both team members absent - a 4 point loss will occur
Expectations of Members
  • Proper Golf attire at all times: No tee-shirts or cut-offs
  • Turn in your score slips in the bar/lounge - no extra holes may be played.
  • Turn in pre-printed match sheets, with totals and signatures by each team after 9-hole match is completed
  • Without a completed slip a forfeit will occur to each team for that nights match
  • Upon completion of play, return all golf carts to the cart staging area. Do not leave carts in parking area.

Rules of Play

Play by the rules of golf - if not listed.
DIVISION I & II

Play the ball as it lies - you may move and place on grass on hole #5 (front dirt area, & hole #8 (Left Rough Area - Find Grass). All Areas where loose dirt is spread.

DIVISION III & IV

You may move the ball up to 24" (except in hazard area, behind trees, and the putting surface) without penalty. You can improve your lie - not your line of flight or swing path.

Full League
  • All divisions play from the "White" tees - If your age and handicap add up to 80 or higher, you may play from the "red Tee Box". This will aid in play enjoyment and a faster pace of play - you can not mix tees.
  • Maximum score on any hole is Triple Bogey (3 over par) - pick up your ball and move to the next hole.
  • Par 3 holes are cart path only - 90 degree rule
  • No Carts within 30 yards of putting surface or surrounding mounds
  • Always repair your ball marks on greens / fill divots with sand
Out of Bounds
  • Cart path to hole #1, when hitting from #9 tee.
  • Maintenance roadway between holes #8 & #11
  • If the ball crosses or touches roadway/cart path its OB - 1 stroke penalty - not distance, keeps pace of play.
Lateral Hazards

Play the ball as it lies, or take an unplayable lie, using 2 club lengths relief from hazard area - No closer to the hole - take 1 stroke penalty

Water Hazards

If ball goes in a water hazard, drop a ball where it crossed into the water, no closer to the hole. Take a 1 stroke penalty. Cleaned out material from ditches is considered ground under repair.

Free Relief
  • All planter bed areas, newly planted trees, fences and manmade objects, ground under repair areas, and tree roots (12" no closer to the hole).
  • Maintenance Area (holes #7, #8, #11) 2 club lengths from the area with no penalty.
  • You may move ball from all footprints (in sand bunkers)- no penalty.
  • Any ball hitting a handrail/bridge must be replayed - no penalty
Substitutions

One (1) substitute per team allowed to win points. No two (2) subs allowed on one (1) team to win points, must designate one (1) team member absent.
